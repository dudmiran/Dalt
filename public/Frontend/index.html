Your Questions Answered!

What is AI-assisted dining?

AI-assisted dining is like having a personal chef who knows your taste buds better than you do! It curates the best food options based on your preferences and location.

How do I rate my meal?

After devouring your meal, simply click on the star rating system and let your voice be heard! Your feedback helps others find their next favorite dish.

Can I leave feedback?

Absolutely! We thrive on your feedback. Share your culinary adventures and help us improve our offerings.

What cuisines are available?

From Kiwi classics to international flavors, we’ve got it all! Explore a world of taste right here in Hamilton.

How does location feature work?

Our AI pinpoints your location and suggests nearby eateries that will make your taste buds dance!
